## 1. User Roles Overview

The system provides specialized features based on your assigned role:
- **Guest**: Browse rooms, make bookings, reserve pool slots, and manage personal profiles.
- **Receptionist**: Manage check-ins/check-outs, oversee all reservations, and manage room statuses.
- **Housekeeping**: Monitor assigned cleaning tasks and update room cleanliness statuses.
- **Manager**: Access analytics, assign tasks, manage room types, and export revenue reports.
- **Administrator**: Full system access including holistic user management and platform configuration.

## 2. Guest Guide

### Booking a Room
1. **Search**: On the homepage, enter your desired Check-in and Check-out dates, along with the number of adults and children.
2. **Select**: Browse the available rooms. Click on a room to view its amenities, high-quality images, and details.
3. **Hold**: Once selected, the system will place a **15-minute hold** on the room. You must complete your booking within this timeframe.
4. **Confirm**: Provide your details and confirm the booking. You can view your invoice in the "My Reservations" section.

### Pool & Spa Reservations
As a guest, you can book time slots for the Infinity Pool & Spa:
1. Navigate to the **Pool Reservation** section.
2. View available 1-hour slots. 
3. Select an open slot and confirm your reservation. Note that pool capacity is automatically enforced.

### Profile Management
Access the **Profile Settings** to update your contact information, upload a profile picture, and review your notification inbox.

## 3. Receptionist Guide

### Dashboard & Daily Operations
The Receptionist Dashboard provides an overview of today's arrivals, departures, and current occupants.
- **Check-in**: Select a confirmed booking when the guest arrives and click "Check In". This changes the room status to OCCUPIED.
- **Check-out**: Select an occupied room and click "Check Out". This automatically triggers a notification and task for the Housekeeping department and sets the room to DIRTY.

### Managing Pool Access
- Access the **Pool Management** panel to view current pool occupancy, manually close the pool, or set it to "Maintenance".
- During the End of Day Procedure (after 9:00 PM), use the dedicated button to hand over the pool area to Housekeeping.

## 4. Housekeeping Guide

### Task Management
Housekeeping staff have a mobile-optimized dashboard tailored for quick updates:
1. **View Tasks**: Check the **Housekeeping Dashboard** for a list of assigned or pending rooms that need cleaning.
2. **Update Status**: 
   - When starting, mark the room as **CLEANING**.
   - Upon completion, mark the room as **CLEAN**. This will automatically return the room to the AVAILABLE pool for the Receptionist.

### Reporting Issues
If a room requires maintenance (e.g., broken AC, plumbing issues), update the status to **OUT_OF_SERVICE** and add a detailed note. This alerts management immediately.

## 5. Manager Guide

### Analytics & Reports
1. **Dashboard**: View high-level KPIs including revenue, occupancy rates, and recent reservations.
2. **Revenue Exports**: Navigate to the Reports section to export operational data. You can download stylized **Excel (.xlsx)** or **PDF** files containing annual and monthly revenue breakdowns.

### Staff & Facility Management
- **Task Assignment**: Delegate specific cleaning or maintenance tasks to individual housekeeping staff.
- **Room Types**: Add, edit, or disable room categories (e.g., Deluxe, Suite). Adjust baseline pricing or update room photos.
- **Amenities**: Manage global amenities that can be attached to various room types.

---

## 6. Administrator Guide

Administrators have access to all Manager and Front-Desk features, plus exclusive system settings.

### User Management
- View all registered users in the system (Guests and Staff).
- Change user roles (e.g., promoting a Receptionist to Manager).
- Block or unblock users based on administrative needs.

### System Oversight
- Administrators receive overarching system notifications.
- Use the overarching notification management to "Clear All" or "Mark as Read" instantly.

---

### Need Help?
If you encounter any issues or errors in the application:
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Look out for toast notifications (red alerts) that explain why an action failed.
3. Contact the IT support desk or refer to the Admin for escalated platform issues.
